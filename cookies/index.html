Cookies Policy

Cookies are small pieces of text sent to your browser by a website you visit. They help that website remember information about your visit, which can both make it easier to visit the site again and make the site more useful to you.

The web application Photo Reducer (compress.mobso.in) uses non-persistent cookies i.e. session cookies. Session cookies serve technical purposes of providing user friendly & seamless navigation. These cookies are only available during an active browser session. These cookies do not collect any information on users and they are deleted as soon as you leave the website. (They are deleted when you close the browser.)
